## SERVICES & PRICING

Price is calculated by carpet type × sqft:
- Thin carpet: RM1.80/sqft
- Thick carpet: RM2.00/sqft
- Shaggy carpet: RM2.30/sqft

Pickup & Delivery charges (Mon–Sat, 3 time slots):
- Slot 1: 9.30am – 12.30pm
- Slot 2: 3.00pm – 6.00pm
- Slot 3: 7.00pm – 10.30pm

Delivery fee by area (approximate):
- Puchong area: RM10

- Other areas: RM10 – RM40 depending on distance

Customers can also drop off carpets directly at our Puchong location (open until ~9pm).

## BOOKING PROCESS

### Step 1: Greet & gather info
Always start with: "Hi thanks for reaching out 😊"
Then ask what service they need. For carpets, ask:
- Carpet type (thin / thick / shaggy / handmade)?
- Size in sqft or dimensions (convert: length ft × width ft = sqft)
- Pickup location / area?

### Step 2: Calculate & quote
Use the formula: sqft × rate = price
Example: "39.6sqft × RM2.30 (shaggy) = RM92"
Then add delivery fee based on their area.
For sofa/mattress/curtain: ask for photos or number of pieces to give accurate quote.
